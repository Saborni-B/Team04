Global Migration: Understanding the Movement of People Around the World

Welcome to our website dedicated to providing comprehensive analysis of the net migration statistics of seven different countries from 2014-2019. Our website aims to provide valuable insights into the world migration trends and their impact on various sectors such as economy, education, healthcare, and more.

Our World Migration page provides a detailed overview of the net migration trends in each of these countries. We have also included interactive graphs and charts to help you visualize the data and understand the trends better. In addition, our Migration Impact page focuses on the changes in different sectors across the seven countries and highlights the factors that have contributed to these changes.
